Happy Huffmans

The Happy Huffmans website is the wedding website for bride Jennifer Daniel and groom Russell Huffman (me). All of the wedding guests and party members will be able to find all necessary information pertaining to the wedding on this website.

View the project here

The Design:

The website needed to not only contain the information for the wedding, but also maintain the same look as the wedding invitations and, when the date arrives, the wedding itself. The website offers all the information guests and wedding party members may need to know about the event in a graphically pleasing and easily navigated way.
