My GA Sketchbook

The idea was To create a website for the artist Ron Huffman that showcases his abilities as a sketch artist, and allow for the purchase of art prints.

The Design:

Ron Huffman is a Georgia resident, passionate historian, and pencil artist that has been sketching for over 30 years. Ron decided he wanted a website made that would showcase his work as an artist and also serve as a platform for potentially selling his artworks.
