Munchkin Counter Web App

I love the Munchkin card game, but I found it difficult to keep track of levels, gear, and strength without a digital aid. There are apps for mobile and windows, but as a mac user, I didn't really have an option that would allow me to display the whole game board's stats all at once. So I decided to make this app to fix that!

View the project here

The Design:

I wanted to create an app that would keep track of levels, gear, and strength for all players during the game. The app allows for up to 6 players (original Munchkin is up to 6 players). Players and names can be easily added or subtracted through the user interface. The aesthetic attempts to match that of the real Munchkin board.

Project Needs:

  1. a web app
  2. add player icon
  3. up to 6 players
  4. simple user interface
