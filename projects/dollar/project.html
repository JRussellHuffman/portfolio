Dollar Bill Buying Power

Money has many uses and abilities, both good and bad. I want to showcase various dualities of the buying power of money. In this case, I am showcasing prosperity and violence on the two sides of the dollar bill. The front side of the dollar shows a city, which I am using to represent the way in which money brings prosperity. The back side of the dollar depicts a helicopter and soldiers, which I am using to represent destruction and violence. Both scenarios exist because we had the buying power to make them happen.

View the project here

The Design:

This mini installation on any US $1 is triggered by the app that contains the art piece. By creating and distributing this art piece as a mobile app, the piece can be viewed by anyone anywhere, assuming they have the app and a dollar, without being contingent on location and without defacing or altering US currency.

Technologies Used:

  1. Unity for app development and packaging
  2. Vuforia for Augmented Reality SDK
  3. Sketchup for creating 3D assets
  4. good ol' pen and paper for creating 3D assets

Project Needs:

  1. a mobile app
  2. the use of an augmented reality SDK
  3. to activate on a $1 USD bill
  4. hand drawn visual assets
  5. 3D modeled scenes generated from the hand drawn assets
