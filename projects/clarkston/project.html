Visualize Clarkston

a public involvement website to coincide with the Streetscape and Pedestrian Enhancement Project.

View the project here

The Idea:

Streetscape improvements and pedestrian walkway enhancements are coming to Clarkston and the city wishes to keep the community involved. The Visualize Clarkston website is one of the main methods of keeping the community informed and involved.

The Design:

Clarkston, Georgia is a refugee resettlement community on the outskirts of Atlanta with vast ethnic diversity. In April of 2014 the City of Clarkston began the planning stages of a Streetscape and Pedestrian Enhancement Project which will result in significant improvements for the town center and adjacent roadways. The City of Clarkston commissioned a website that would keep the community informed and allow them to get involved.
