Who I am

My name is Joel Russell Huffman but most people call me Russell. I am an designer, developer, and artist based out of Atlanta, Georgia. My purpose in life is to be creative and I do so in everything I do, whenever I can. When I am not writing javascript or scribbling design ideas, I am cooking, gardening, or playing guitar. The process of doing and creating drives me. I absolutely love working on unique projects and I am grateful that I can help others through my creativity.

Being creative is my passion. When I was 16, I discovered my love for the visual arts and eventually came to study fine art at GCSU. College afforded me the opportunity to explore all mediums, where I discovered that working on the computer allows me to best express myself. After graduation I worked in interpretation design working with cultural history and storytelling. This lead me to graduate school where I studied Digital Media at Georgia Tech, where I recently graduated. I love working with storytelling, cultural history, civic engagement, and education applications of new media. If any of this sounds interesting to you, please reach out to me! I love talking about my passions.
